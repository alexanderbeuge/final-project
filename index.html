ABOUT ME

My name is Alexander Beuge a.k.a. Beugism. I am an illustrator by night and a designer by day. I always wanted to be a painter since I was little. I spent my early years drawing triangle-shaped trees and horses – loads of horses. At one point I even told my Mum that I wanted to be a horse when I grew up. Later I obsessively drew various versions of Batman, whose limbs looked more like sausages than anything else.
